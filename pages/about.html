About MarketPlace

Pakistan's most trusted online classified marketplace

Our Story

MarketPlace was founded in 2024 with a simple mission: to make buying and selling easy, safe, and accessible for everyone in Pakistan. What started as a small platform has grown into one of the country's largest online classified marketplaces.

We connect millions of buyers and sellers across all major cities including Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Faisalabad, Multan, and Peshawar. From cars and mobile phones to furniture and jobs, MarketPlace is the go-to destination for all your buying and selling needs.

Our platform is designed with safety and trust in mind. We verify listings, provide secure messaging, and offer tips to ensure safe transactions. We're committed to creating a marketplace where everyone can trade with confidence.
